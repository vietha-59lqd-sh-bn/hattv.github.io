Hi, I’m River! A Public Relations student passionate about storytelling, brand communication, and social media culture. I’m particularly interested in how brands build emotional connections with audiences through content, community, and everyday digital interactions. Through my experiences working with small brands and student projects, I enjoy turning ideas into engaging stories that resonate with people online.

**Industry:**

* **Interior Design Brand – Social Content Development**: Developed a narrative-driven social media approach for an interior design company by shaping content through the perspective of a designer sharing ideas about home aesthetics and lifestyle. Created storytelling-based posts while curating product-focused content on Facebook and Instagram, helping maintain a cohesive brand voice and visual identity across platforms.

* **Cotu Store – Social Media & Influencer Collaboration**: Managed social media presence for a start-up specializing in custom-designed teddy bears. Produced Instagram and TikTok content aligned with brand identity, planned content schedules, and handled daily community interactions. Coordinated KOL/KOC outreach and collaborations to expand brand visibility and audience engagement.

* **PicMe Photobooth – Event Promotion & Customer Engagement**: Supported marketing activities for photobooth services targeting student events and small parties. Generated content ideas and social media posts, contacted potential clients through digital channels, and assisted with on-site operations during events to ensure smooth customer experiences.

**Leadership & Community:**

* **Han Thuyen Book and Action Club – Head of External Relation**: Led partnership development with more than 15 local organizations and coordinated multiple community projects promoting reading culture and youth engagement. Managed sponsorship communication and supported event collaboration initiatives.

* **CBN Photography and Design Club – Specialist Department Member**: Participated in creative photography and visual design projects while co-organizing 5 large-scale events with over 300 attendees and 8 workshops on photography and design for high school students in Bac Ninh.
